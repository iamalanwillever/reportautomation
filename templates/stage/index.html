Team,

 

During the PSaS All-Employee Town Hall yesterday, we shared the intent to integrate RxTPL into RxCrossroads by McKesson 3PL. This strong union provides a “one-stop shop” to our biopharma (generics) customers enabling them to commercialize their products. RxCrossroads by McKesson will strengthen our collaboration efforts with industry partners and include an unmatched service offering, which is supported by one of the largest and most trusted organizations in healthcare.

 

To that end, we have asked Ben Maizel and Kevin Kissling to lead this effort and they will share more in the coming weeks. In the meantime, we look forward to joining our greatest asset—our people—with industry best practices and services to create even more value for our customers. This decision aligns with our Playbook strategy of enterprise-first mindset and is the right thing to do for our customers and our company.

 

Thank you for the great work you do every day and more to come on this exciting offering.
